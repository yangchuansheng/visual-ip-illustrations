## Risks and Mitigations

- Identity drift: repeat the full `gopher.png` marker set in every operational file and make `gopher-ip.md` the identity authority.
- Logo confusion: keep Go logo identity outside the positive Go Gopher route identity and treat logo-forward output as a QA failure.
- Endorsement drift: use `source-reviewed` and source/license wording from `source.md`, with official affiliation, approval, sponsorship, and endorsement only as boundary language.
- Passive mascot placement: require Go Gopher to perform the central cognitive article action, and make the image lose meaning when Go Gopher is removed.
- Route leakage: keep Xiaohei, Littlebox, Tom, Ferris, Seal, and OpenClaw identities outside Go Gopher prompts, QA, and delivery wording except in leakage repair instructions.
- Public sample timing: keep public generated Go Gopher samples behind release review.
- Phase bleed: keep controller changes, public docs, metadata, validator expansion, Node tests, and release evidence in their mapped phases.
