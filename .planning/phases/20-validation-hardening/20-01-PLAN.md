Existing validator contracts to preserve and extend:
- `defineCheck(id, message, run)` creates ordered checks rendered by `runChecks()`.
- `Results.render()` prints `[PASS|FAIL|SKIP] {id} {message}` and `Summary: total={n} passed={n} failed={n} skipped={n}`.
- `assertIncludes(text, relativePath, markers, relation)` and `assertNoMarkers(text, relativePath, markers, relation)` produce actionable failure messages with paths and missing or forbidden markers.
- `sealosFixedMarkers()` returns the full uploaded-image marker list.
- `parsePublicSealosSampleApproval(releaseChecklistText)` parses Sealos public asset approval.
