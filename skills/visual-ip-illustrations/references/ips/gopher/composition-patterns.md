## Composition Families

Draw a loose left-to-right or curved path. Go Gopher operates the decisive handoff point: pointing to a turn, carrying a key, sorting envelopes, bridging stepping stones, repairing a broken link, stamping a checkpoint, or routing a small signal through the path.

### System Local View

Draw only 3-5 core parts. Go Gopher works inside one decisive part: inspecting a lens, untangling threads, weighing claims on scales, shielding a fragile idea, tuning a lamp, repairing a lever, or connecting two shelves.

### Before/After
